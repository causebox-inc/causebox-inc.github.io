    More About Us

    Our Mission

    At 'CauseBox', we believe that individuals seek to find purpose by making an impact on the world. Causebox hopes to make this possible, by connecting volunteers/student activists, non-profit organisations, and corporates on a platform that erases social barriers and provides opportunity for passionate individuals. Our platform encourages the sharing of ideas, innovation, and best practices, so that making a difference in this world is far from impossible.

    Support Generated Broadcast

    Our platform allows the youth to broadcast their cause solely based on the support they gather rather than monetarily boosting their page.

    Organic Growth

    Causes with similar aims in the same area will be connected in hopes to merge and create a stronger cause.

    Visibility

    To be visible and have the ability to influence One needs to be at the Site of power. In this day an age the site of power lies with the youth, online, in social media.
